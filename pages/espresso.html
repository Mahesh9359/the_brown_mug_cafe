Espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans. It’s the base for many other coffee drinks, like lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos.Here’s a breakdown of the details, ingredients, and a simple recipe:

Ingredients

  1. Espresso beans (finely ground)
  2. Filtered water

Equipments

  • Espresso machine
  • Coffee grinder
  • Tamper
  • Portafilter

Recipe

  1. Preheat the espresso machine
  2. Grind the espresso beans to a fine powder.
  3. Dose the coffee
  4. Tamp the grounds
  5. Brew the shot
