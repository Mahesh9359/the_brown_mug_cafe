A latte is a smooth and creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of microfoam. Itâ€™s a bit milkier than a cappuccino and has a more delicate flavor profile, making it a favorite for coffee lovers who prefer a subtler coffee taste.

Ingredients

  1. Espresso beans (finely ground)
  2. Milk
  3. Cocoa Powder

Equipments

  • Espresso machine
  • Coffee grinder
  • Milk frothing pitcher
  • Espresso cup or mug

Recipe

  1. Brew the espresso
  2. Steam the milk
  3. Pour the milk
  4. Add flavor or garnish
  5. Brew the shot
